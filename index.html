The non-negotiables

Across all thirty days — no exceptions

  • Never script yourself word for word. Bullet points only — you're great off the cuff.
  • Post daily on IG. Even a story counts. Consistency beats perfection.
  • Respond to every DM personally. You're building the pipeline, not the algorithm.
  • Lead with elegance transforming your life — never with politics or COVID.
  • The divorce stays private. Full stop.
  • Never mention competitors by name or frame. Your story is the differentiation.
  • Every piece of content moves someone one step closer to buying — or it doesn't get posted.
